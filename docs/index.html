There was quite a bit of freedom to carve out your own specialization within music. From there I honed in more on the crossover between music and technology exploring digital electronics with Arduinos, learning about manipulating audio with C, and creating music training tools with JS. That's more or less how I got into programming/web development.

Additionally, I like making little electronic trinkets here and there.
